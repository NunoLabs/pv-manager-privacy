Privacy Policy – PV MANAGER

PV MANAGER is an application designed to help users organize product catalogs, calculate totals, and prepare purchase lists.

This application is NOT an online store and does NOT sell products. 
No purchases, payments, or transactions are made within the app.
All purchases must be completed through official external websites or authorized distributors.

Information Collection and Use
PV MANAGER does not collect, store, or share personal data with external servers. All information entered by the user is stored locally on the device.

Data Storage
The app may store locally:
- Product selections
- Purchase lists
- Order history
- App preferences

This data never leaves the user’s device.

Advertising
The app may display ads for non-PRO users using third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies.

User Control
Users can clear app data at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing local storage.
